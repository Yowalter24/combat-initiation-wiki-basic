ACCESSORIES

aka "Hats" — wearable items that grant perks or pure vanity.
Accessories are Combat Initiation's main form of character customization. Each Core Accessory costs points from your budget of 3 — pick wisely! The Bloxmaster Headband adds a 4th point at a steep cost. Hats are primarily earned through achievements and enemy indexes. Sorted below by effect category for easy gearing.

WEAPONS

7 base weapons · 14 upgrade paths · pick 2 at round start.
Weapons are your main tool for wrecking enemies. Pick 1 Primary + 1 Secondary at round start (100 TIX each; +50 TIX per weapon already owned). Each weapon has two mutually exclusive upgrade paths bought from the Shop during intermission. Choose wisely — you can't swap mid-run.

GEARS

Stage rewards · picked every 10 waves · 4 categories.
Gears are tools you obtain at the end of each stage (every 10 waves). You're given a choice of 4 gears by default — one from each category. Once you hit the cap for a category, that category stops appearing. Gears range from passive healing buffs to powerful mobility options.

⌨ DEFAULT KEYBINDS

GActivate Active gears
RActivate Refresher gears
QActivate Utility — Grappling Hook
SPACEActivate Jet Boots & Umbrella

Most keys are rebindable in Settings — except Space (not listed).

★ PRO TIPS ★

GEAR PICK PRIORITY

S-TIER

🥇 UNIVERSAL CORE

Grappling Hook + Cheezburger + Epicsauce + Teddy Bloxpin

The "can't go wrong" loadout. Mobility + heal + damage amp + safety net. Works on every campaign, every weapon combo.

S-TIER

🧨 BOSS KILLER

Jet Boots + Bloxiade + Decoy Deploy + Red Stratobloxxer

Mobility for dodging telegraphs, Bloxiade for CD spam during Epicsauce frames, Decoy pulls aggro, Stratobloxxer punishes long combos. 💀 bosses.

A-TIER

🧟 MINION MASTER

Zombie Staff + Battle Armor + Reflector + Witches Brew

Pair with Dark Conjurer or Meeboid hat for guaranteed Zombie Staff. Reflector protects your squishy army. Witches Brew = rapid summons.

A-TIER

🛡 TANK MODE

Umbrella + Slateskin Potion + Battle Armor + Regen Coil

125 temp HP + 50 max HP + 1.5%/s regen. Slateskin slam dunks = crowd nuke. New players love this one.

A-TIER

🧛 LIFESTEAL LOOP

Grappling Hook + Bloxy Cola + Epicsauce + Teddy Bloxpin

Cola = 2x lifesteal for 30s. Pair with Samurai Helmet (+100% lifesteal) and Katana for a self-healing murder machine.

B-TIER

🌀 REFLECTOR CHEESE

Jet Boots + Witches Brew + Reflector + Teddy Bloxpin

Requires Gourmet Chef Hat to shine. Reflector + Witches Brew CD = near-permanent shield uptime. Trivializes ranged-heavy waves.

⚠ PICK ORDER TIP: Always prioritize Teddy Bloxpin on hard campaigns — one free life is priceless. Grab Grappling Hook turn 1 if offered unless you've specifically committed to Jet Boots. And remember: Witches Brew sacrifices cognitive being — use at your own risk.
